BloomDemic

This JavaScript & JQuery visualization mimics the spread of Viruses, Bacteria, and Parasites
It is designed to also test the limits & efficiency of JQuery DOM Manipulation with self-propagating JS objects that re-assign classes
    Viruses spread the fastest, but have the highest death rate;
     

    Bacteria spreads at a speed slower than Bacteria, but has a lower death rate;
     

    Parasites spread painfully slowly, but almost never experience cell death;
